# Scisprint 2022 April in Taipei

We cordially invite you to join the Scisprint 2022 April in Taipei (at a
[cozy meeting room](#venue)). We will be using 4 hours to discuss and
code for the following projects:

- Python 官方說明文件臺灣繁體中文翻譯計畫:
  [https://github.com/python/python-docs-zh-tw](https://github.com/python/python-docs-zh-tw)
- modmesh: [https://github.com/solvcon/modmesh](https://github.com/solvcon/modmesh)

If you want to join and bring your projects, please [Contact
us](#contact-us).

## Meeting Minutes and Registration

Add your name to the sprint note: [https://hackmd.io/@sciwork/HJ_gzk-Gq](https://hackmd.io/@sciwork/HJ_gzk-Gq)
.

## Date & time

9th April, Saturday, 2022, 14:00 \-- 18:00

## Cost Sharing

Attendees will pay for the venue fee (2280 NTD) together.
Note that we have 17 people in the last sprint :)

## Requirements

A laptop to get your hands on. Some snacks if you like. Stay healthy!
Check out the [Cost Sharing](#cost-sharing), too.
