## About Project

**Python Official Document Traditional Chinese Translation** ([project
link](https://github.com/python/python-docs-zh-tw))

We are a passionate and dedicated volunteer group committed to bridging
the language gap in the Python community. Our primary mission is to make
Python\'s official documentation accessible to the vast Traditional
Chinese audience by providing accurate and comprehensive translations.
Whether you are a seasoned Python developer or a language expert, we
welcome individuals who are enthusiastic about Python and passionate
about making knowledge accessible to all!
